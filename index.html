My Last Holiday

Hello world! Welcome to my first webpage 😎

During my last holiday, I visited Dubai. As it is winter right now in Switzerland, summer and sun was much needed. Dubai is a city that never sleeps. There is always something to do, whether it's shopping, dining, or exploring the desert. I spent my days lounging by the pool, visiting the Burj Khalifa, and enjoying the vibrant nightlife. We visited the following attractions:

  • Burj Khalifa
  • The Dubai Mall
  • Aura Skypool
  • Miracle Garden

Burj Khalifa

  • Height: 828 meters (2,717 feet)
  • Floors: 163
  • Observation Deck: Located on the 148th floor, offering panoramic views of Dubai

The Dubai Mall

  • Size: Over 1,200 retail stores
  • Attractions: Dubai Aquarium, Ice Rink, VR Park
  • Dining: Over 200 food and beverage outlets

Aura Skypool

  • Location: Located on the 50th floor of the Palm Tower
  • Features: Infinity pool with stunning views of the Dubai skyline
  • Access: Exclusive to hotel guests and members

Miracle Garden

  • Size: Over 72,000 square meters
  • Floral Displays: Over 50 million flowers arranged in various themes
  • Season: Open from November to April
